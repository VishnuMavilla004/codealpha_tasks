Credit Scoring Model Summary
----------------------------

Objective:
To predict whether an individual is creditworthy (1) or not (0) based on financial data.

Models Used:
- Logistic Regression
- Decision Tree Classifier
- Random Forest Classifier

Best Performing Model:
- Random Forest with high accuracy and ROC-AUC score.

Evaluation:
- Confusion Matrix: Shows classification accuracy.
- ROC Curve: Demonstrates model's performance in classifying positive/negative cases.

Usage:
This model can be used in finance applications to automate credit checks.
